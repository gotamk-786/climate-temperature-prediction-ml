# Research Paper Folder

This folder contains the material needed to write and submit the Data Science course research paper for the climate change project.

## Files
- `research_paper_draft.md`: main paper draft with all required sections
- `technical_research_proposal.md`: proposal version for topic approval or early submission
- `paper_submission_map.md`: quick guide showing where implementation, results, and comparison are covered
- `paper_outline.md`: short writing structure for quick planning
- `literature_review_notes.md`: notes for related work and citation direction
- `methodology_justification.md`: explanation of regression choice, chronological split, and EDA scope
- `limitations_and_improvements.md`: limitations and future upgrade ideas
- `references.md`: references and source links
- `submission_checklist.md`: final paper readiness checklist

## Writing Rule
Do not write generic claims in the paper. Every result must come from actual notebook output, tables, or figures.
